EXPENSIVE CARS

1. 1955 Mercedes-Benz 300 SLR Uhlenhaut Coupe – $143 million

  • Price: $143 million (sold at auction in 2022)
  • Engine: 3.0-liter 8-cylinder engine
  • Top Speed: 180 mph (290 km/h)
  • Description: This one-of-two prototype car was sold at an auction for a record-breaking price, setting a new benchmark for rare car sales. It's known for its racing heritage and stunning engineering, and the model is now considered an iconic piece of automotive history.

2. 2023 Rolls-Royce Sweptail – $28 million

  • Price: $28 million
  • Engine: 6.75-liter V12 engine
  • Top Speed: 155 mph (250 km/h)
  • Description: Custom-built for a specific customer, the Sweptail is a one-of-a-kind creation by Rolls-Royce, featuring bespoke design elements. It took nearly five years to develop and incorporates unique materials, such as rare woods and glass from a historic Rolls-Royce collection.

3. Bugatti La Voiture Noire – $18.7 million

  • Price: $18.7 million
  • Engine: 8.0-liter quad-turbocharged W16 engine
  • Top Speed: 261 mph (420 km/h)
  • Description: La Voiture Noire ("The Black Car") is Bugatti's ultra-luxury hypercar, and the most expensive new car ever produced. Combining exceptional speed, design, and engineering, it is a tribute to Bugatti's legacy and a statement of wealth.

4. Pagani Zonda HP Barchetta – $17.5 million

  • Price: $17.5 million
  • Engine: 7.3-liter V12 engine
  • Top Speed: 214 mph (345 km/h)
  • Description: The Zonda HP Barchetta is an extremely rare, limited-edition car with only three units built. Its unique design features a rear wheel fairing and an open top, and it combines extreme performance with avant-garde aesthetics.

5. Ferrari 250 GTO (1962) – $70 million

  • Price: $70 million (auction record)
  • Engine: 3.0-liter V12 engine
  • Top Speed: 174 mph (280 km/h)
  • Description: While the Ferrari 250 GTO was originally sold for much less, its value has skyrocketed over time. Known as one of the most iconic Ferraris ever made, it is extremely sought after by collectors, and one of the most expensive cars ever sold at auction.
